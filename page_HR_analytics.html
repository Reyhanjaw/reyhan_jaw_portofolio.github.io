HR Analytics

An HR analytics dashboard is a visual representation of key human resources data and metrics that provide valuable insights to HR professionals and decision-makers. It allows them to monitor and analyze various aspects of workforce performance, recruitment, employee engagement, retention, and other HR-related metrics in real-time or over specific time periods.
The HR analytics dashboard's main objective is to empower HR professionals and organizational leaders with data-driven insights to make informed decisions about workforce planning, talent acquisition, employee development, and overall HR strategy. By visualizing complex HR data in a user-friendly and easily digestible format, the dashboard helps HR teams identify patterns, trends, and areas for improvement, ultimately leading to better-informed decisions and more effective human resources management.
